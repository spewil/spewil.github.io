about me

I live in islington, london and go on lots of adventures.

I grew up in moultrie, georgia and worked on cool cars.

I went to MIT where I studied mechanical engineering and film theory.

I made cool stuff with awesome people in the center for bits and atoms.

I wrote my undergraduate thesis in the MIT nonlinear dynamics lab about internal waves.

I was awarded a Marshall Scholarship for two years of study in the UK. I wrote a master's thesis at Cambridge about the mathematics of origami. I wrote another master's thesis at Imperial about control theory in random networks.

Thanks for stopping by. Feel free to reach out.
